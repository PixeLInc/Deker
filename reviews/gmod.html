Garry's Mod

Mark L | October 17th, 2017
A simple, yet amazing game. It allows for the creation of anything the user could imagine. Developers can come together and make an insane game-mode that others can play and experience for themselves and the best part is, it's easy.
Users can join a sandbox world, creating awesome structures and machines to do what they'd like, or join a server with their friends and kill off enemies in a team-based gamemode.
The game was released back in 2004 but is still striving and being bought TO THIS DAY. It is very popular among all age groups and can be considered the best sandbox, customizable game so far. Some of the more popular gamemodes are:
